ABOUT US

Get to know us!

We are a NGO that aims to provide universal access to food and other facilities to the needy people.

Our mission is to provide sustainable health and education services to children and adults throughout the world without personal, religious, or political prejudice.

One of Helping Hands primary functions is to provide volunteer opportunities for medical professionals, medical students, and non-medical volunteers to do their best.

Building schools and hospitals, providing health care through mobile medical and dental clinics...for over 25 years we have helped the people all over the globe.
